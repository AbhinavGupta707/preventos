**Who we are.** PreventOS ("we") is operated by TBD(owner: legal entity). We are the data
controller for the information described here. Contact: TBD(owner). You can complain to the
UK Information Commissioner (ico.org.uk) at any time.

**What PreventOS is — and is not.** PreventOS offers programmes that help you change
habits around smoking, vaping, drinking, and sleep. It is a wellbeing product. It is not a
medical service and we do not provide medical care.

## What we collect

- **Account details:** your email address and sign-in information.
- **Programme information you give us:** your answers to programme questionnaires, the
  things you log (cravings, drinks, sleep diary entries, plans, chat messages with the
  coach), and your goals. **This is health information and we treat it with the highest
  level of protection the law provides.**
- **Wearable data — only if you turn it on:** sleep and step data from Apple Health or
  Health Connect. Off by default; you choose each data type; you can turn it off at any
  time and it stops immediately. Your diary always takes precedence — wearable data never
  overrides what you tell us.
- **How you use the app:** events like screens opened and notifications received, so we can
  improve the product and understand whether the programmes work.

## How we use it — and your choices

Everything below runs on your **explicit consent**, asked for separately, in plain terms,
when it becomes relevant — never buried in terms you scrolled past. You can withdraw any
consent in the app's consent centre and the related use stops immediately. Saying no to any
optional purpose never locks you out of your programme.

| Use | Can you decline it and keep using the app? |
|-----|--------------------------------------------|
| Running the programmes you enrol in | Declining means not using that programme |
| Suggesting another programme based on two short screening questions | Yes |
| The AI coach (your messages are processed by an AI service on our behalf) | Yes — full programmes work without it |
| Reading wearable sleep/step data | Yes |
| Product analytics | Yes |

**If you may be in crisis.** Safety messages and crisis resources are built into the app and
work even offline. If you tell us something that suggests you may be at risk, a scripted
safety flow runs and a trained person may review it. This is the one situation where we may
process your information to protect your life, even mid-conversation. AI is never in charge
of that decision.

## What we never do

We never sell your data. We never use it for advertising. We never share programme
information with employers or insurers. Wearable data is never used for anything except the
sleep features you can see.

## Who helps us run the service

Carefully chosen providers process data on our instructions: sign-in (Clerk), AI coach
responses (Anthropic), notifications (Expo), analytics (PostHog), and hosting
(TBD — UK region). Some are in the United States; where they are, we use the UK's approved
transfer safeguards. Your coach conversations are sent without your name or email attached.

## How long we keep it

While your account is active, plus TBD(legal) months. You can delete your account in the
app: your data is erased except the minimal safety-escalation records we are required to
keep for accountability, and anonymous statistics that can no longer identify you.

## Your rights

Access, export (there's a button in the app), correction, deletion, restriction, objection,
and withdrawal of consent — all free. We respond within one month. Age: PreventOS is for
adults (18+). TBD(owner): confirm final age policy.

## Changes

We will tell you in the app before any material change takes effect, and ask again for
consent where the law requires it.
