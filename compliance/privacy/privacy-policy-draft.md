**Who we are.** PreventOS ("we") is operated by TBD(owner: legal entity). We are the data
controller for the information described here. Contact: TBD(owner). You can complain to the
UK Information Commissioner (ico.org.uk) at any time.

**What PreventOS is — and is not.** PreventOS offers wellbeing programmes that help adults
change habits around smoking and vaping in the consumer beta. It is not a medical device,
does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition, and does not provide
medical care or emergency care.

## What we collect

- **Account details:** your email address and sign-in information.
- **Programme information you give us:** your answers to programme questionnaires, the
  things you log (cravings, vaping/smoking progress, plans, chat messages with the
  coach), and your goals. **This is health information and we treat it with the highest
  level of protection the law provides.**
- **Wearable data:** not collected in the QuitKit + Exhale consumer beta. If Apple Health
  or Health Connect import is added later, it will be off by default, separately consented,
  and covered by an updated wearable-data DPIA before use.
- **How you use the app:** events like screens opened and notifications received, so we can
  improve the product and understand whether the programmes work.

## How we use it — and your choices

Everything below runs on your **explicit consent**, asked for separately, in plain terms,
when it becomes relevant — never buried in terms you scrolled past. You can withdraw any
consent in the app's consent centre and the related use stops immediately. Saying no to any
optional purpose never locks you out of your programme.

| Use | Can you decline it and keep using the app? |
|-----|--------------------------------------------|
| Running the programmes you enrol in | Declining means not using that programme |
| Suggesting another programme based on two short screening questions | Yes |
| The AI coach (your messages are processed by an AI service on our behalf) | Yes — full programmes work without it |
| Push reminders and proactive check-ins | Yes |
| Reading wearable sleep/step data | Not used in consumer beta |
| Product analytics | Yes |

**If you may be in crisis.** Safety messages and crisis resources are built into the app and
work even offline. If you tell us something that suggests you may be at risk, a scripted
safety flow runs before any AI response. Where support coverage exists, a person may review
the safety record. PreventOS is not an emergency service and is not monitored for urgent
help. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call 999 or your local emergency number.

## What we never do

We never sell your data. We never use it for advertising. We never share programme
information with employers or insurers. Wearable data is never used for anything except the
sleep features you can see.

## Who helps us run the service

Carefully chosen providers process data on our instructions. The beta processor register is
`processor-register.md` and includes sign-in (Clerk), notifications (Expo), optional AI coach
providers (Fireworks or Anthropic), support tooling (TBD), and hosting (TBD — UK/EEA
preferred). Some providers may be outside the UK; where they are, we use the UK's approved
transfer safeguards. Coach conversations are sent without your name or email attached.

## How long we keep it

We keep each type of data according to `retention-deletion-schedule.md`. You can delete your
account in the app and from a public web link: primary systems should delete or anonymise
your data within 30 days, except minimal safety, security, legal, or anonymised aggregate
records that we are allowed or required to keep. Backups expire on their normal cycle.

## Your rights

Access, export (there's a button in the app), correction, deletion, restriction, objection,
and withdrawal of consent — all free. We respond within one month. Age: PreventOS consumer
beta is for adults (18+) only.

## Changes

We will tell you in the app before any material change takes effect, and ask again for
consent where the law requires it.
